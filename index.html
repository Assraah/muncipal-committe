City Government and Operations

Departments

The Department of Administration is headed by the Chief minister KPk as Chief Executive of the MunicipalMaven and the Health Secretary who is the Chief Operating Officer of the MunicipalMaven. They work jointly to effectively coordinate services that are responsive to citizen needs. City Administration works with the Common Council to identify and solve problems and develop guidelines for municipal operations and services.

Services

The MunicipalMaven is part of a larger partnership in the city of Abbottabad. City services include police and volunteerfire departments,public transportation, city planning, licensesand applications, parks and recreation, the city council and the library services consortium shared between community members in the area.

Business

MunicipalMaven has a volunteer BID (Business Improvement District)sub-commitee organized by the city Abottabad, they meet on Wednesdays at the municipal center between 6-7pm.
